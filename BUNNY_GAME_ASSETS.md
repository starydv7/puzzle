## üí° **CREATION TIPS**

### For Bunny Character:
- Big, friendly eyes
- Soft, rounded features
- Ears that can express emotion
- Body language that shows emotion
- Consistent color scheme

### For Food Items:
- Make them look delicious but simple
- Bright, appealing colors
- Slightly larger than real size (kid-friendly)
- Clear, recognizable shapes

### For Animal Friends:
- Each should have distinct personality
- Similar art style to bunny
- Friendly, approachable
- Different colors to distinguish easily

### For Backgrounds:
- Soft, dreamy quality
- Not too busy (won't distract from game)
- Pastel color palette
- Peaceful, happy atmosphere
